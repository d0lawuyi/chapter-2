Yurts

Our yurts are perfect for nature enthusiasts who want to experience the beauty of the Pacific Trails Resort without pitching a tent. With just enough space for two adults and two children, our yurts provide a cozy camping experience for small families.

Location 1
Our first yurt is located in a secluded grove of redwoods. It features a queen size bed, sofa bed, microwave, and refrigerator.
Location 2
Our second yurt is located near the ocean, with a breathtaking view of the Pacific. It features a queen size bed, gas fireplace, and small kitchenette.
